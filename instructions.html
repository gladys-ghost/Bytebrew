Game Instructions

Objective

Your goal is to eliminate a specified number of enemies in each level. Survive and progress through increasingly challenging stages.

Game Mechanics

- Kill the required number of enemies to unlock the next level's door.

- If you die, you must restart the level from the beginning.

- Each level increases in difficulty with more enemies to defeat.

Combat and Survival

R Reload your weapon

2 Switch to Medkit

E Use Medkit to heal

Progression

Successfully eliminate all required enemies to unlock the door to the next level. Be strategic, stay alert, and survive!
